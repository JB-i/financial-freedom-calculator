How to Read This

Today's rupees means the value of money as you understand it now. This is the easiest number to think with.

Future rupees means the larger-looking amount after prices have gone up over time.

Yearly spending from investments means the part of your investment money you plan to use each year after reaching freedom. A smaller percentage needs a bigger fund.

Simple India Tax Note

The tax inputs are editable because Indian tax rules depend on the investment type and can change. The defaults are only a practical starting point.

West Bengal professional tax is mainly for salary, business, professional, or self-employment income, so this calculator keeps it separate from investment withdrawals.
