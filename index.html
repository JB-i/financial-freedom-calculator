Plain-Language Notes

Real values are in today's rupees, so they show purchasing power. Nominal future values are inflated rupees at the target date.

Safe withdrawal rate is the percentage of your portfolio you assume you can withdraw each year. A lower rate needs more capital but gives a larger cushion.

Tax drag is a simplified annual reduction to returns. It is not a legal tax calculation.

Warnings

  • This is not financial advice.
  • Taxes are simplified and fully editable.
  • Inflation and returns are uncertain.
  • Past returns do not guarantee future returns.
  • Verify Indian tax rules with official sources or a tax professional.
  • West Bengal professional tax matters mainly for active income, salary, business income, or self-employment income.
